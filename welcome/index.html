    Our history

    • 2015

      Our Humble Beginnings

      When we first  formed the club, we had less than ten people.

    • 2016

      Growing in silence

      We grew up in silence, waiting for a chance to bloom.

    • 2017

      Year of evolution

      We changed our way of recruiting and received the Operational Construction Award by MSRA.

    • 2018~2019

      Phase Two Expansion

      The number of our clubs has reached an unprecedented level. At the same time, we have also created many new activities and carried out new cooperation.

    • 2020

      Year of transformation

      We launched the new name "Computer Amateur Association" and transformed into a contact society of MOSC under the guidance of MSRA. Microsoft Open-source Study Community (MOSC), born out of the Microsoft Student Club (MSC), is a further exploration of talent training in Universities by MSRA.
